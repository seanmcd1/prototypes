2 What we changed at the capture step

Six focused changes turn a manual, error-prone photo upload into a guided, auto-captured moment with instant, friendly feedback.

  • 🛈
    Set expectations up front

    A short reassurance strip — "~20 seconds, encrypted, here's why we ask" — before the camera opens, lowering anxiety.

  • 🎯
    Live on-device guidance

    Real-time cues (move closer, reduce glare, hold steady) replace a blind "take a photo" button.

  • ⚡
    Auto-capture when aligned

    The camera fires itself the moment the document is framed and sharp — no perfect tap required.

  • ✓
    Instant quality check

    Sharpness, glare and full-document checks run on-device with specific, encouraging retry guidance — never a generic "rejected".

  • ↺
    No dead-ends

    Easy fallbacks: switch document type, upload from gallery, or continue on another device.

  • ⏱
    Fast, transparent processing

    A live checklist shows progress so the wait never feels like a frozen dead screen.
